Privacy Policy

At MOMOS, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, etc., when you place an order or sign up for our newsletter.
  • Payment Information: Credit card information or payment details when making a reservation or placing an online order.
  • Website Usage Data: Information about your use of our website, including IP address, browser type, operating system, and location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders or reservations.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • To send marketing communications (if you have opted-in) including offers, promotions, and updates about our restaurant.
  • To improve the functionality and performance of our website and services.

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files that are stored on your device and help us to remember your preferences and track your browsing activity.

By using our site, you agree to the use of cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to assist with analytics, payment processing, and marketing. These services may collect information about your interaction with our website. We ensure that any third parties we use comply with relevant privacy laws and regulations.

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and improve user experience.
  • Payment Processors: Payment services such as Stripe or PayPal to process your orders securely.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to ensure the safety of your personal data. These measures include encryption, firewalls, and secure payment gateways. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Withdraw consent for us to process your data (if applicable).

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@restaurant.com.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
