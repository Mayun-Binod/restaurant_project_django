Our Policies

Please review the following policies to understand how we operate.

1. Food Safety and Allergens

We take food allergies seriously. Please inform our staff about any food allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order. While we take steps to prevent cross-contamination, we cannot guarantee the complete absence of allergens.

2. Reservation Policy

Reservations are recommended for large groups or during peak hours. Reservations can be made via our website or by calling our restaurant. A valid credit card is required for group reservations to secure the booking.

3. Delivery and Takeaway

We offer delivery and takeaway services. Delivery times may vary based on location and demand. All orders are subject to availability, and we will notify you of any changes to your order.

4. Conduct in the Restaurant

We strive to provide a welcoming and respectful environment. Any behavior deemed disruptive, unsafe, or disrespectful to staff or other customers may result in removal from the premises.

5. Discounts and Promotions

Discounts and promotional offers are subject to change and may have expiration dates. They cannot be combined with other offers unless specified. Please refer to specific promotional terms for details.

6. Privacy of Data

We value your privacy and protect your personal data as outlined in our Privacy Policy. Your information will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except where required by law.

7. Changes to Policies

Our policies may change from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page. We recommend checking back regularly for the most current policies.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about any of our policies, please contact us at contact@restaurant.com.
